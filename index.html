How it works

1. Install

Add the Slack app; connect Google Drive to create a private “Manager Diary” folder.

2. Use in flow

Use /boost and /diary. Notes save to your Drive; we index metadata only.

3. Get digest

A short weekly email summarizes signals and suggests next best actions.

FAQ

Is this private?

Yes. Notes live in your Google Drive. We don’t store raw Slack/Zoom/Jira content—only derived signals (e.g., participation %, sentiment score).

Do I need another tool?

No. It’s Slack-first with a weekly email. Optional dashboard can come later.
